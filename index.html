Who we are

The Iraqi Student Association of Ottawa consists of passionate Iraqi students from Baghdad all the way to Basra! All of our events are currently held at the University of Ottawa. Our mission statement is to better the student experience of all students, particularly Iraqis, by organizing student activities, fundraisers, and social gatherings!
